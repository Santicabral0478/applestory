Apple Mouse

allows users to control the cursor much more intuitively than previous input devices such as scroll wheels and stylus pens.

Design: Square and angular design, with a beige plastic casing and a black rubber ball at the bottom that allows the movement of the cursor.

Technology: mechanical technology to track the movement of the ball and convert it into movement of the cursor on the screen.

Functionality: Allows users to move the cursor on the screen and click on items.

Compatibility: Compatible with the Apple Lisa and the Apple Macintosh series of computers released in 1984.
