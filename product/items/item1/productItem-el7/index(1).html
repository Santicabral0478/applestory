Macintosh Plus

offers an improved computing experience compared to the original Macintosh, with an advanced operating system, a faster processor, improved RAM, a high-capacity floppy drive, a familiar user interface, and multiple ports for connecting peripherals.

Operating System: MacOS 3.0 operating system.

Processor: Motorola 68000 8 MHz.

RAM memory: 1 MB of RAM memory.

Storage: 3.5-inch floppy drive. it can read and write to 800KB and 1.44MB disks
