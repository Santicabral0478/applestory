Macintosh IIX

An advanced computer, with exceptionally fast performance, large memory capacity, and improved graphics and sound. Very popular among users who need exceptional performance for applications such as graphic design, animation, and video editing.

Processor: Motorola 68030 at 40 MHz, It also has an integrated floating point processing unit (FPU).

Memory: 32 MB of RAM, can be expanded to a maximum of 128 MB.

Graphics and Sound: It includes an improved graphics chip that offered a screen resolution of up to 1152x870 pixels in 8-bit color. It also included a built-in sound card and a sound card slot.

Storage: Two internal SCSI hard drive bays and one integrated floppy drive. It also has several expansion slots for adding external storage drives.
