Macintosh SE

It offers an enhanced computing experience compared to the Macintosh Plus, with an advanced operating system, a faster processor, more RAM, a high-capacity floppy drive, and the option of an internal hard drive. In addition, the Macintosh SE offers the ability to add a video card and connect an external color monitor.

Operating System: MacOS System 3.3, an upgraded version of the MacOS operating system.

Processor: Motorola 68000 8 MHz.

RAM memory: amount of variable RAM memory, 1 MB, 2 MB or 4 MB depending on the configuration.

Storage: 3.5-inch floppy drive that can read and write 800KB and 1.44MB disks.
