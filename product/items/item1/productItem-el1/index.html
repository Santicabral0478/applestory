Apple II

it offers a variety of features, including a built-in monitor, keyboard, DOS operating system, multiple programming languages, graphics and sound, expansion ports, and popular games.

Monitor: Built-in video monitor, connected via a video cable.

Keyboard: built-in keyboard that connects to the central unit.

Operating system: DOS operating system (Disk Operating System)

Graphics and Sound: Ability to display color and black and white graphics, as well as output sounds through a built-in speaker.
