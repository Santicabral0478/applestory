MacBook Air

It offers decent performance for basic use, great battery life, and an impressive design, making it a highly desirable device for those who need a slim and stylish laptop.

Screen: 13.3-inch LED screen with a resolution of 1280 x 800 pixels.

Processor: 1.6 GHz or 1.8 GHz Intel Core 2 Duo processor.

RAM memory: 2 GB of DDR2 RAM at 800 MHz.

Storage: 80 GB or 64 GB of solid state storage (SSD).
