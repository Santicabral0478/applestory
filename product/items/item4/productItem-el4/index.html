iPad

It offers a unique and easy-to-use user experience that focuses on web browsing, media, and applications, making it a highly desirable device for those looking for a portable and versatile way to consume digital content.

Screen: 9.7-inch screen with a resolution of 1024 x 768 pixels.

Processor: 1 GHz Apple A4 processor.

RAM memory: 256 MB of RAM memory.

Storage: 16 GB, 32 GB or 64 GB of internal storage.
