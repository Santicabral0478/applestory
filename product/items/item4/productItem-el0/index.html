iPhone

the first iPhone model released by Apple. Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity, available in 4GB or 8GB versions. Its operating system was iPhone OS 1, based on macOS X.

Screen: 3.5 inches with a resolution of 320 x 480 pixels and multi-touch technology

Processor: Samsung ARM single core 620 MHz

RAM memory: 128MB

Internal storage: 4 GB or 8 GB.
