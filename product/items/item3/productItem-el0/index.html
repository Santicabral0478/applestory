iMac G4

well received by consumers and trade press due to its innovative design and advanced hardware specifications

Design: Elegant and futuristic design in the form of a desk lamp, with a 15-inch or 17-inch LCD screen.

processor: 1.25 GHz PowerPC G4, 256 MB of RAM and an ATI Radeon 9200 graphics card with 32 MB of VRAM.

Storage: 60, 80 or 160 GB on an internal hard drive.

Connectivity: USB 2.0, FireWire, Ethernet, Wi-Fi and Bluetooth ports.
