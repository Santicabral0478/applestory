Mac mini

popular with both home users and business environments. Its compact size and minimalist design have made it a popular choice for those looking for a desktop computer that doesn't take up too much space.

Processor: Intel Core 4 to 6 cores.

Memory: RAM up to 64 GB.

Storage: 2TB SSD.

Ports: HDMI, Thunderbolt 3, USB-A, Ethernet, headphone jack.
