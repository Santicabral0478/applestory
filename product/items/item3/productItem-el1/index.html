iBook G4

it offers good performance in a compact and lightweight design, with a long-lasting battery and built-in wireless connectivity. A good option for users who need a laptop for everyday tasks such as browsing the Internet, writing documents, and managing email.

Processor: 1.0 to 1.33 GHz PowerPC G4 processor.

Display: 12 or 14-inch LCD screen with a resolution of 1024x768 pixels.

Storage: 30, 40, 60 or 80 GB hard drive.

RAM memory: 512 MB of DDR SDRAM RAM memory expandable to 1.5 GB.
