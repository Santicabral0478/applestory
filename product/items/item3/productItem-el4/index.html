iPod Mini

very popular due to its compact size, its ample storage capacity and its elegant and colorful design. In addition, it was one of the first portable music devices to offer a long battery life and a color screen.

Storage: 4 GB or 6 GB storage capacity

Display: 1.67-inch color LCD

Battery: rechargeable battery with a duration of up to 8 hours of playback.

Connectivity: Connection via USB 2.0 for data transfer
