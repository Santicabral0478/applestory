iPod

great success due to its storage capacity, its ease of use and its elegant design. In addition, it established the business model of iTunes and the online music store, allowing users to purchase and download songs directly to the device.

Storage: 5 GB of storage, which allows you to store up to 1000 songs in MP3 format.

Touch Scroll Wheel: Allows you to navigate your music library and adjust the volume.

Screen: 2-inch monochrome screen

battery: rechargeable battery, provides up to 10 hours of continuous playback.
