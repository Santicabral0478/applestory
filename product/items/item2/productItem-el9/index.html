Power Macintosh G3

A significant upgrade to Apple desktop computers, offering increased performance and processing power, increased storage capacity, and better image quality compared to previous generations of Macintosh.

Processor: Motorola PowerPC G3, clock speed ranges from 233 MHz to 450 MHz.

Memory: RAM up to 1 GB.

Storage: internal hard drive up to 18 GB.

Graphics: Includes ATI Rage graphics cards with a maximum screen resolution of 1024x768 pixels.
