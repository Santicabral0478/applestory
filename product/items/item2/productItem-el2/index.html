Macintosh IIFX

designed for professional users who required high performance and expandability. The equipment presents a rectangular design in a dark gray tone, with an industrial aesthetic that suited its professional nature.

Processor: Motorola 68030 40 MHz.

Memory: 8 MB RAM, expandable up to 128 MB.

Storage: 40 MB internal SCSI hard drive.

Graphics: Dedicated graphics chip, the "SuperDrive" that can display 256 colors simultaneously at a resolution of up to 1152x870 pixels.
