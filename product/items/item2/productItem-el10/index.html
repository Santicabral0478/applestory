iBook

A compact, colorful laptop designed for everyday use, including web browsing, email, word processing, and other everyday tasks.

Processor: Depending on the model, PowerPC G3 or G4 processors that ranged in speed from 300 MHz to 1.42 GHz.

Screen: 12.1-inch active matrix screen, 800x600 or 1024x768 pixel resolution.

RAM memory: between 32 MB and 1 GB of RAM memory.

Storage: Hard drives with capacities ranging from 3 GB to 60 GB, and some models also included a CD or DVD drive.
