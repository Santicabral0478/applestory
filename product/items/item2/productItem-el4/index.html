Macintosh LC 575

Compact and powerful, offering a faster processor, plenty of RAM, and a high-resolution display, making it ideal for everyday tasks like word processing, web browsing, and image and sound editing

Processor: Motorola 68040 33 MHz.

RAM memory: 4 MB ram, expandable up to 36 MB,

Storage: 80 MB, expandable with external drives.

Screen: 14-inch screen with a resolution of 640x480 pixels.
